Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

Data Collection

HoneyMix ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not track your usage history or harvest your data.

Data Storage & Backups

All data entered into the App—including your custom ingredients, recipes, and batch history—is stored entirely locally on your device.

We (the developers) do not have access to your database or your recipes.

If you utilize the App's "Backup Database" feature, the resulting file is managed by your device's native Files app. If you have iCloud Drive enabled on your device, this backup may sync securely to your personal Apple account, which remains completely inaccessible to us.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a premium unlock ("HoneyMix Pro") and optional tip features. All transactions are securely processed entirely through Apple’s App Store standard payment system. We do not have access to your credit card information, billing address, or Apple ID details.

Third-Party Services

The App does not utilize any third-party analytics, advertisements, or tracking services.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at the email listed above.
