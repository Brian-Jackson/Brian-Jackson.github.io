Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 11, 2025

Data Collection

SwarmTracker ("the App") does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not track your movements, usage history, or harvest data.

Location Services

The App requests access to your device's GPS location solely to:

  • Display your current position on the map relative to your equipment.
  • Calculate the distance to your traps for route optimization.
  • Allow you to save the geographical coordinates of new trap deployments.

This location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any external server.

Data Storage

All data entered into the App (including trap locations, inspection logs, equipment lists, and photos) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We (the developers) do not have access to your database.

Third-Party Services

The App utilizes Apple Maps to provide map tiles and navigation services. Your usage of the map features is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. The App does not utilize any other third-party analytics or tracking services.
